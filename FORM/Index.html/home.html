Rules for Loan Approval

  1. Active Contribution for six months consecutively
  2. Applicant must have passed attendance test of 57%
  3. Submission of BVN, NIN, a Passport Photograph which is not older than 6 months
  4. Fill loan application form & bond after successful interview by an Exco member
  5. Loan Amount is times two of the first ten or three of second balance on ledger balance of members of thrift savings balance
  6. At least two members as loan guarantors who are active members who have paid for half of the loan

No member may be granted any loan unless

  • He/she has returned a completed Loan Application Form setting out the purpose for which the loan is being sought and accompanied with a repayment plan and surety of two guarantors
  • He/she produces two sureties who must be a staff member of
  • He/she has been a full member for at least 6 months, except in certain parts of the month or in case of paying an amount not less than 60% of the total saving balance in lump sum, subject to the approval by the General Meeting

No member shall be entitled to receive loan if

  • He/she has not fully repaid the earlier loan given to him/her
  • He/she is indebted to the society in any way
  • He/she has stopped making monthly contribution of the Social Savings Account of the society
